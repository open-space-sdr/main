No trademark license is granted by this repository.

“ScaleRF,” “QuadRF,” “MoonRF,” related logos, product names, domain names, trade dress, and branding are trademarks, service marks, or product identifiers of Scale RF Inc.

## Permitted nominative references

You may make accurate nominative references to Scale RF, QuadRF, or MoonRF where permitted by law and where the reference does not imply sponsorship, certification, approval, origin, compatibility, or endorsement.

Examples of generally acceptable wording:

- “Derived from the released Scale RF MoonRF antenna design files.”
- “Based on released Scale RF antenna files.”
- “Uses design files originally published by Scale RF under CC BY-SA 4.0.”
- “Unofficial derivative of the released MoonRF antenna design.”

## Restricted uses

You may not use Scale RF marks, logos, names, product names, or trade dress to imply that your product, derivative, service, repository, documentation, or campaign is official, certified, approved, sponsored, endorsed, manufactured by, tested by, or supported by Scale RF unless Scale RF gives written permission.

Examples of wording that requires permission or should be avoided:

- “Official MoonRF product.”
- “Scale RF certified.”
- “QuadRF compatible” if compatibility has not been approved or verified by Scale RF.
- “MoonRF-certified array.”
- Use of Scale RF logos on packaging or product pages for a third-party product.

## Product and project naming

Do not name a derivative product, repository, company, domain, or campaign in a way that is confusingly similar to Scale RF, QuadRF, MoonRF, or related Scale RF marks.
