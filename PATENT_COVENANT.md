# Patent Notice, Defensive Patent Covenant, and Reciprocal Patent Grant

## 1. Purpose

This document describes a limited patent covenant provided by Scale RF Inc. for the Released Antenna Design identified in this repository. It applies only to the released antenna design files and does not apply to the QuadRF RF Board, QuadRF RF Tile, RF transceiver core, RF-core schematics, RF-board layouts, Gerbers, bills of materials, manufacturing outputs, FPGA designs, firmware, software, calibration systems, production test systems, or unreleased Scale RF designs.

The goal is to allow open study, modification, manufacture, use, and sale of hardware based on the released antenna design files while preserving a reciprocal defensive patent framework for Scale RF Inc., QuadRF, MoonRF, contributors, manufacturers, distributors, customers, users, and the broader released-design ecosystem. The goal is not to release or license the QuadRF RF Board / Tile or other Scale RF RF-core products for reproduction or manufacture.

This Patent Covenant is separate from the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License (`CC BY-SA 4.0`). It does not modify the CC BY-SA 4.0 license and imposes no condition on the exercise of rights granted under CC BY-SA 4.0.

## 6. Recipient Reciprocal Patent Grant and Defensive Non-Assert

As a condition of accepting, relying on, claiming, invoking, or receiving the benefit of the Scale RF Limited Patent Covenant, each Recipient grants Scale RF Inc. and the other Protected Parties a worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive patent license and defensive covenant not to assert under Recipient Patent Claims for any Protected Scale RF Activities.

This reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert includes the right, under Recipient Patent Claims, for Scale RF Inc. and the other Protected Parties to make, have made, use, sell, offer for sale, import, export, distribute, modify, develop, test, operate, service, support, document, and commercialize the Protected Scale RF Activities.

This reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert applies only to Recipient Patent Claims that would be infringed by Protected Scale RF Activities. It does not grant Scale RF Inc. or any other Protected Party a license to unrelated Recipient technology, unrelated products, unrelated services, unrelated patent claims, or Recipient implementations that do not practice, embody, support, manufacture, test, calibrate, control, document, service, or enable the Released Antenna Design or a Compliant Derivative.

This reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert is non-exclusive. The Recipient remains free to practice, commercialize, license, sell, assign, enforce, and otherwise exploit its Recipient Patent Claims outside the scope of this section.

This reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert is intended to prevent a Recipient from accepting the benefit of Scale RF's patent covenant while using its own patents, or patents controlled by its affiliates, to block Scale RF or the protected released-design ecosystem from practicing, supporting, improving, manufacturing, selling, or commercializing the Released Antenna Design, Compliant Derivatives, or Scale RF implementations that practice, embody, support, manufacture, test, calibrate, control, document, service, or enable the Released Antenna Design or Compliant Derivatives.

The reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert in this Section 6 is intended to survive termination of the Scale RF Limited Patent Covenant as to Recipient Patent Claims that became subject to this Section 6 before termination, except to the extent survival is not permitted by applicable law.

Termination does not affect rights that cannot be terminated under applicable law, and does not terminate the Recipient's reciprocal patent grant and defensive non-assert under Section 6 to the extent Section 6 survives termination.
