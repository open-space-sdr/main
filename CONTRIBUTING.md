# Contributing

Scale RF welcomes issue reports, documentation corrections, test results, build notes, simulation improvements, and community discussion related to the released antenna design files and selected debugging documentation.

Because this repository includes hardware design files, patent-sensitive subject matter, RF safety issues, and possible export-control considerations, contribution handling is more careful than in a typical software-only project.

## Contribution policy

By submitting a contribution, you represent that:

1. you have the right to submit the contribution;
2. the contribution does not include confidential, proprietary, export-controlled, or restricted technical data that you are not authorized to disclose;
3. the contribution does not knowingly infringe third-party intellectual-property rights;
4. the contribution may be distributed under the same license as the files it modifies, unless Scale RF expressly agrees otherwise in writing;
5. to the extent your contribution would otherwise be covered by patent claims that you own or control, you grant Scale RF and downstream users a royalty-free, non-exclusive patent license and covenant not to assert sufficient to make, have made, use, sell, offer for sale, import, modify, and distribute the contribution as part of the released antenna design.

## Substantial contributions

Scale RF may require a separate contributor agreement before accepting substantial contributions, including contributions involving:

- antenna geometry;
- RF feed networks;
- RF-board interfaces;
- pogo-pin or spring-contact structures;
- calibration methods;
- phased-array control methods;
- manufacturing processes;
- enclosure integration;
- simulation models;
- firmware, FPGA, or software implementing RF control or calibration;
- QuadRF RF Board / Tile schematics, layouts, manufacturing data, or RF-core implementation details;
- export-sensitive or safety-sensitive material.

Scale RF may decline or remove contributions for any reason, including legal, safety, export-control, patent, trademark, product-roadmap, manufacturability, regulatory, quality, or maintenance reasons.

## No restricted technical data

Do not submit materials that are classified, controlled, export-restricted, proprietary to an employer or third party, subject to a nondisclosure agreement, or otherwise not yours to release.

Do not submit technical assistance, design details, or operating instructions for restricted military, intelligence, weapons, electronic-warfare, countermeasure, missile, controlled radar, targeting, or other prohibited or controlled applications. Do not submit RF-board layouts, Gerbers, BOMs, manufacturing packages, clone designs, or derivative RF-core product designs unless Scale RF has expressly requested and authorized that contribution in writing.

## Attribution and third-party material

If your contribution incorporates third-party material, clearly identify the source, license, and required attribution. Do not submit third-party material unless its license is compatible with this repository.

## Relationship to the Patent Covenant

Contributions may also be subject to the reciprocal patent-grant and defensive non-assert terms in `PATENT_COVENANT.md` if the contributor claims, invokes, relies on, or receives the benefit of the Scale RF patent covenant for the Released Antenna Design or a Compliant Derivative.
