# Third-Party Notices and Attribution

This repository includes released antenna design files and related documentation for the QuadRF and MoonRF antenna architecture by Scale RF Inc.

## Upstream antenna work

The radiating patch geometry and hybrid feed are based in part on the Triple Feed Patch Antenna by Maarten Baert and Robin Theunis.

Reference:

> Maarten Baert and Robin Theunis, et al., “Highly broadband circular polarized patch antenna with 3 phase feed structure,” 2017 11th European Conference on Antennas and Propagation (EUCAP), IEEE, 2017.

Upstream design files (January 2019):

https://files.maartenbaert.be/quadcopters/
